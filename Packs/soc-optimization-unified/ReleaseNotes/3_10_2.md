#### Scripts
##### SOCEnrichFromList
- **New**: Data-driven Upon Trigger enrichment engine. Resolves `SOCFrameworkEnrichmentMap_<LIFECYCLE>` by naming convention, gathers values from each lane's source paths under `SOCFramework.Artifacts.*`, and fires the corresponding built-in reputation command (`|||<lane>`) per non-empty lane. Read-only by definition; no shadow_mode, no dataset writes.

#### Playbooks
##### Foundation - Enrichment_V3
- **Refactored**: Collapsed from 15 hard-coded lane tasks reading dead `SOCFramework.Artifacts.{IP,File,Domain,URL}` paths into a 3-task thin dispatcher (start -> SOCEnrichFromList -> Done) delegating to the new engine. Adding a new lifecycle or lane is a list-row change only — never edits this playbook.
